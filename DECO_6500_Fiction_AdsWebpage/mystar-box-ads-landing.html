What is MYSTAR BOX?

MYSTAR BOX redefines how people engage with cultural heritage. By connecting physical exhibits with digital exploration, it transforms ordinary visits into personalised journeys.

Each scan opens a story-rich box, revealing artefacts, missions, and collectible rewards that merge curiosity with discovery.

Available at participating museums and libraries worldwide.

For Museums and Libraries

MYSTAR BOX enables cultural institutions to activate their collections through accessible AR storytelling. Museums can host interactive missions, encourage deeper visitor engagement, and gather insight into participation patterns.

Partnering institutions receive easy onboarding, aggregated analytics, and flexible curation tools that enhance public connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MYSTAR BOX a game or an educational tool?

It is both. The app encourages exploration while maintaining an educational focus through curated AR missions that connect you with authentic cultural narratives.

Can I use it without internet?

Scanning works offline after initial connection. Some content may require data for updates and accessing the full library of digital collectibles.

Is content the same for everyone?

Experiences differ by venue and membership tier. Higher tiers unlock deeper narratives, exclusive missions, and rare collectible artefacts.

Experience quality, story access, and artefact availability depend on membership level.

Certain missions, narratives, and collectibles are location-based or time-limited.

All content is curated for clarity, inclusivity, and engagement.
